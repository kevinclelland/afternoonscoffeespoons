Bio: With skilled musicianship, original songwriting and over a thousand gigs around Southern Africa since 2009, The Kickstands pride themselves on being able to perform at intimate venues as well as major festivals and showcase events. We’ve had the privilege of performing alongside some of South Africa’s most popular artists such as Goldfish, Just Jinjer, Goodluck, Albert Frost, Arno Carstens, Matthew Mole, Gangs of Ballet, Desmond and the Tutus and Mango Groove. Without radio support, our live performances have carried our unique, improvised and energetic acoustic sound around Southern Africa gaining fans along the way, all the while fine-tuning our craft … most of all, enjoying the journey.
